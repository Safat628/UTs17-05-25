Compliance with International Standards

Traceability is not just a benefit; it's increasingly a requirement. UtshoTracer helps you comply with critical international standards and regulations.

  • EU Regulation (EC) No 178/2002: Mandates full traceability of food products within the European Union for safety and recall purposes.
  • FDA FSMA (USA): The US Food Safety Modernization Act requires traceability for high-risk produce to prevent foodborne illnesses.
  • Global Market Demands: Many markets including Japan, South Korea, and the UAE, increasingly demand compliance with GlobalG.A.P. and digital traceability solutions.
  • EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR): Requires proof that imports are deforestation-free and fully traceable.
  • GFSI Standards: Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI) standards benchmark global food safety systems, which rely heavily on traceability.
  • Sustainability Certifications: Certifications like Rainforest Alliance, Organic, and Fair Trade require traceability to verify ethical and environmental compliance.
